AXFX is a multi-effect plugin in the AX73 family — modulation, delay, and reverb effects designed to pair with the AX73 polysynth but operable on any audio source. The effects draw from the same era of hardware reference as Martinic's instrument plugins (late-70s to mid-80s rack effects), keeping the catalogue aesthetically coherent rather than mixing modelling registers.
