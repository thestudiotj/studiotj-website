AX Chorus is a standalone analog-chorus plugin in the AX73 family — modelled on a vintage analog chorus circuit rather than designed as a digital chorus algorithm. The effect pairs aesthetically with the AX73 lineup but works on any audio source; the modulation depth and stereo width track the original's behaviour rather than offering modern extended ranges.
