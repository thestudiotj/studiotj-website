Picks is a curator channel for the brands that earn an evergreen recommendation — researched, used, or both. The categories cover the tools of the craft (photography, video, audio, writing, drawing, design, software, workspace, site and workflow, print) and the surrounding cultural register that StudioTJ and The Subtext Lab share an interest in (gaming as a subject, not just an activity). Each brand routes through Impact, with disclosure built into every page.
