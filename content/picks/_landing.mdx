Picks is a curated set of brands — one page per brand, organized by category. Photography, drawing, software, print, site and workflow, workspace. Each entry is a brand StudioTJ has researched, used, and cleared for an affiliate relationship through Impact. The selection is built on fit. The affiliate link is how that fit turns into a commission.

Six categories. One principle: the brand earns its place through quality and honest coverage. What belongs, stays. What only makes noise, does not.
