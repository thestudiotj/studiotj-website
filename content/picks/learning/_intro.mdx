Learning is where the craft gets taught. The brands in this category run the courses and structured instruction behind the skills the rest of the site takes as given — photography and post-production first, then the wider creative disciplines that feed the work. The register is practitioner-led and project-based: instruction from people who do the thing, aimed at a skill you can take straight to the camera, the edit, or the page.
