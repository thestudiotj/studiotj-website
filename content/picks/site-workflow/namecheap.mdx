Namecheap is a domain registrar and web hosting provider founded in 2000. The product set covers the full lifecycle of a domain — registration, DNS management, WHOIS privacy, and renewal — alongside shared and VPS hosting, SSL certificates, and business email. WHOIS privacy is included at no additional cost, which most registrars charge for separately.

The contrast worth naming: Namecheap's pricing structure does not inflate the first year to obscure the renewal cost. The renewal price is visible upfront, and the gap between first-year and renewal is smaller than the industry norm. For a studio running several domains across projects, that transparency compounds.
