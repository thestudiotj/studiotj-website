SmallRig makes camera accessories for working video shooters — cages, top handles, follow focus systems, mattes, support rigs, and the power and audio gear that turns a single body into a kitted setup. The catalog runs across Sony, Canon, Nikon, Fujifilm, Panasonic, Blackmagic, and Leica systems, with model-specific cages cut for current bodies rather than universal mounts.

The detail worth naming is how the catalog grows. SmallRig runs a program called DreamRig that co-designs products with creators in its community — the result is a lot of small, single-purpose accessories that exist because someone shooting a specific camera asked for one. The Leica SL3 cage, machined in titanium and developed in cooperation with Leica itself, sits at the upper end of the same approach. The brand reads as mass-market on the catalog page; the manufacturing reads otherwise when the product is in hand.
