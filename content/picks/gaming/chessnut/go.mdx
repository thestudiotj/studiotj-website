The Go is the Chessnut built for travel. Magnetic pieces hold their position on a moving board — train tray-tables, plane seatbacks, café tables that get bumped — without losing the chip-based full piece recognition the rest of the Chessnut lineup runs on. It folds into a carry bag and connects to Chess.com or Lichess over Bluetooth from a phone, no laptop required.

## Position in the lineup

The Go is the smallest and most travel-ready Chessnut. The trade against the Air and Air+ is piece interchange — Go pieces are magnetic and don't share with the rest of the lineup, so a Go owner adding an Air later runs two separate piece sets. The trade is worth making if the board needs to survive a backpack.
