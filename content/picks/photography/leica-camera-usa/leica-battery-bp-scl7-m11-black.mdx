The BP-SCL7 in black is the current M11-platform battery. Lithium-ion, 1,800mAh, USB-C charging compatible via the M11 base. It powers the M11, M11-P, M11-D, M11 Monochrom, and M EV1 — every current M-mount body Leica ships.

## Position in the lineup

A spare BP-SCL7 is the practical purchase for anyone working an M11-family body across a long day. The black finish is the standard variant — Leica also ships the BP-SCL7 in olive and silver to match camera body finishes; the black is the most universal. Same battery across five bodies means one spare covers the entire current M lineup.
