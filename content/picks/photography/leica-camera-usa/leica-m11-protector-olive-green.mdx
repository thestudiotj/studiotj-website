The M11 Protector in olive green is a leather case that wraps the M11 body — base plate, sides, front — leaving the lens mount, viewfinder, and rear controls accessible. The olive green finish is Leica's safari colorway, drawing on the M3 and M4 olive-green editions originally produced for press and military photographers in the 1960s.

## Position in the lineup

The M11 Protector is for someone who wants daily-use leather protection on an M11 body without committing to a full ever-ready case. Within Leica's accessory range it fits the entire M11 platform — M11, M11-P, M11-D, M11 Monochrom, M EV1. The olive green finish reads as a deliberate visual choice rather than the default — paired with a black or silver M body it draws on Leica's military-press lineage; paired with the Glossy Black, it reads as cross-era cosmetic conversation between 1962 and now.
