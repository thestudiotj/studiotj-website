The Halfcase Q3 in cognac is Leica's leather half-case for the Q3 platform. It wraps the bottom half of the body — base plate, grip area, lens base — in cognac-finish leather while leaving the controls, viewfinder, and screen fully accessible. The base opens for battery and SD card access without removing the case from the camera.

## Position in the lineup

The Halfcase Q3 is for someone who carries the camera daily and wants the leather-and-brass aesthetic that the Q3's textured paint surface doesn't deliver on its own. Within Leica's accessory range it sits alongside the standard black and olive green half-cases and the full-coverage Ettas pouches — the half-case is the working compromise between protection and access. Cognac develops a patina with use that pairs the case visually with the Glossy Black and brass-finish accessories across the Leica catalog.
