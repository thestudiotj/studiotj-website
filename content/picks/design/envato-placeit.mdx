Placeit is a mockup generator and design template library run by Envato. Drag a design onto a t-shirt template, a phone case, a tote bag, or a mug — the platform produces a photo-realistic product shot ready for an Etsy listing or social post. The library extends beyond mockups: logo templates, video intros and outros, social media graphics, podcast covers, and the assorted visual assets a print-on-demand or small-creator business runs through every week. A recent addition extends the photographed-model library with AI-generated apparel models, broadening the range of body types and settings a single subscription covers.

The detail worth naming is what Placeit is and isn't. It is not a substitute for shooting your own product photography — a real photo of the product on the right body in the right light beats a generated mockup every time. It is a substitute for not shipping anything because there's no time to shoot two hundred design variants across base products. For print-on-demand operators working at scale, the tradeoff between custom imagery and shipping speed is real, and Placeit is the tool that makes the math work on the speed side.
