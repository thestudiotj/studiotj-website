# C-type Lustre Pro

C-type Lustre Pro is silver halide photographic paper at the archival professional tier — exposed by RGB laser in a colour lab, finished with a lustre surface that holds detail across the full tonal range. Not only a paper — a print made by the photographic process the medium grew up on.

## The paper itself

C-type is the lab process photography has used for the last half-century. The paper is light-sensitive silver halide; the image forms when the paper is exposed to controlled RGB laser light, then developed in chemistry. The result is a photographic print in the literal sense — formed by light. The lustre finish carries a soft sheen that gives the image presence and reads clean across the room.

## What it carries

C-type Lustre Pro carries work where sharp detail and tonal range matter most — architectural lines, contrast-driven compositions, images that depend on the precision of edge and the depth of colour. The lustre surface gives those qualities their full register.

## Longevity

Silver halide prints on archival professional paper hold their colour for decades. Keep the print out of direct sunlight, frame it behind UV-filtering glass if it sits in a bright room, and the print holds its character through the years it hangs.

Every C-type Lustre Pro print is produced through Prodigi's archival professional lab in the UK, EU, or US, whichever is closest to your delivery address.
