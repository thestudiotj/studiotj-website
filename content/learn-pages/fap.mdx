# Framed Art Print

A framed art print is a Photo Rag print finished for the wall — a Classic frame in black, white, or natural oak, glass glaze across the face, the print filling the frame edge-to-edge. Not only a print — a photograph framed at the gallery standard.

## The frame

The Classic frame is a clean rectangular profile in solid wood, available in black, white, or natural oak. The frame sits flat against the wall. The depth carries the print forward to read as framed work. The profile is slim, sized to sit alongside other pieces. Each frame ships assembled with the print already mounted inside, glass glaze fitted, hanging hardware attached.

## The print inside

Every framed art print is built around a Hahnemühle Photo Rag print — 308gsm pure cotton, smooth matte surface, archival pigment inks. The print sits directly behind the glass glaze, filling the frame edge-to-edge. The [Photo Rag page](/shop/learn/hpr) carries the full detail on the paper.

## What it carries

Framed art prints carry the same work Photo Rag carries — Atmospheric and Halcyon collections, and Signature work that calls for it. The Classic frame in any of its three colours gives those photographs a finished form for the wall: frame, glaze, hardware, ready to hang.

## Longevity

Archival pigment on cotton rag behind glass holds its colour for decades. The glaze protects the print from dust, fingerprints, and ambient handling. Keep the framed piece out of direct sunlight; the colour holds across decades.

## How we make it

Every framed art print is produced through Prodigi's Fine Art Trade Guild-approved facilities in the UK, EU, or US, whichever is closest to your delivery address. Photo Rag printed flat, frame assembled by hand, print mounted, glass fitted, hardware attached. The piece ships boxed and ready to hang.

A frame and a print as one piece.
