Clipped to the talent, the Mic Mini transmitter sends audio to the Multifunctional Module on the 7P, which routes it to the phone alongside the stabilised footage and the native tracking. The audio half of the gimbal rig — three things in one workflow, no extra cables.

Came in the 7P Vlog Combo, which is the right way to buy it if the gimbal is the use case — the bundle prices both parts cheaper than buying separately. Ten-gram transmitter, magnetic and clip mount options, omnidirectional capsule, eleven-hour battery on the transmitter, and the OsmoAudio protocol that pairs directly with most current DJI cameras and gimbal modules without needing a separate receiver.

The standalone receiver came in the box and lives in the case for now. Most contexts where it would matter — camera-mounted use, USB-C plug into a laptop — are already covered by other rigs in the kit, so the receiver is held in reserve rather than working daily.
