The 7P came in the Vlog Combo with the Mic Mini and the Multifunctional Module — the magnetic tracking unit that handles subject tracking, Mic Mini reception, and a fill light from the side wheel. The combination upgrades everything phone-related at once: stable handheld footage, native tracking that works in the phone's own camera app without forcing the Mimo app, and clean audio routed through the receiver instead of the phone's internal mic.

Bought partly as a test of whether a dedicated Pocket camera would earn its place in the kit. It did — the Pocket 4 sits in the bag now — but the 7P doesn't get retired by it. The phone is what's already in the pocket, and the 7P is what makes phone footage worth keeping. Built-in extension rod and tripod, ten-hour battery, USB-C charging passthrough to the phone while the module is acting as receiver.

The same Mimo-app friction the Pocket 4 has shows up here too — fiddly when you do need it for the more advanced modes. For the native-tracking workflow, you can mostly stay out of the app.
