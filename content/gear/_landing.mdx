These are the tools the work runs on. Every piece in the kit was chosen for a specific job and stayed because it does that job well — used long enough to know its limits, not just its specs.

The kit covers the whole pipeline, not just the camera. Bodies and primes for capture, lenses chosen for the way they actually render rather than the chart they top. Software for the edit, the catalogue, the export to print or stock or web — the bench where most of the hours actually go. Accessories that solve the small problems that show up every shoot. Computer and storage that keep the archive intact and the workflow honest, the part nobody markets and the part that matters most when something fails.

Each piece earns its place. Some by being the cleanest answer to a specific need, some by being the one that's still here after years of trying alternatives, some by being so reliably out of the way that the work happens through them and not despite them.
